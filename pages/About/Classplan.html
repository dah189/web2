MSIS HCC-Plan Of Study

This is a smart pages for student or his/her advisor to make a study plan for the Master of Science in Information Science in Human-Centerd Computing Specilization. You can select what you want to enroll in. When you add your first course, you will be required to enter student name and advisor name. Then you can add or drop the course and make own plan.
